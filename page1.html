Cxevalo

This is the basic word to denote a horse. The only one you need to remember.

Cxevalino

To make a noun feminine, simply insert -in-. at the end.

Cxevalido

To refer to the child of something, insert -id- at the end.

Cxevalidino

You can combine both suffixes to speak about a "female baby horse".
